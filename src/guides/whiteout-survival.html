Quick Tips from Experts

Useful tips and tricks from top Whiteout Survival players to improve your gameplay immediately.

Hero Selection

Always prioritize upgrading your main hero first. Focus on one hero type at a time rather than spreading resources across multiple heroes.

Helpful for 98% of users Verified Tip

Resource Production

Keep resource buildings at equal levels for balanced growth. Focus on food and wood early game, then transition to metal and fuel as you progress.

Helpful for 95% of users Pro Strategy

Research Priority

Research T4 troops as soon as possible. The combat advantage they provide is worth the resource investment and can be game-changing for alliance warfare.
