Jeronimo

Exploration Skill:

Jeronimo executes multiple powerful slashes, each dealing up to 224% of his attack. These chained strikes make him effective in dealing sustained damage to high-health enemies.

Expedition Skill:

Even when not deployed, he grants a 25% damage buff to all troops, making him a top-tier support pick.

Why Pick:

Jeronimo is an offensive powerhouse and his team-wide buff scales well through Gen 7, making him a must-have for both new and experienced players. Use him in almost all compositions for general damage boosts.

Natalia

Exploration Skill:

Natalia specializes in disruption. She has abilities that stun enemies and boost her team's defenses, providing solid crowd control.

Expedition Skill:

She enhances troop defense and has a chance to stun enemy units during expeditions.

Why Pick:

Natalia is especially F2P-friendly and offers great utility with her control mechanics. Ideal for defensive formations and PvE exploration modes.
